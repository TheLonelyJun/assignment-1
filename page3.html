About Coach Carter

Coach Carter is a 2005 American biographical teen sports drama film starring Samuel L. Jackson and directed by Thomas Carter. Coach Carter was based on a true story. The plot is about this Richmond High school's basketball team, The Richmond Oilers. Coach Carter somone who has played for The Richmond Oilers when he was young was hired to be the Coach of disrespectful youngsters. He was hired to make the now losing team of Richmond to winning team.
