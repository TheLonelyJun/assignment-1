About God of War (2018)

God of War (2018) is an action-adventure game developed by Santa Monica Studio and published by Sony Interactive Entertainment. It's based on the Greek God Kratos leaving his past of vengeance against the Gods of Olympus and now lives in the realm of Norse Gods.

About Rainbow six siege

Rainbow Six Siege is an online tactical shooter video game developed by Ubisoft Montreal and published by Ubisoft. It's a five on five game where one team is attacking and one is defending. Each side has operators that each time can pick that can help them attack/defend their objective.
